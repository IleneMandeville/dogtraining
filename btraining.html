General Information

The purpose of the Behavioral dog training
style is to solve the problem of dog’s bad
behavior. This is also known as “behavioral
modification.” This training style is the
first type of training the puppy is exposed
to. There are different methods of this style
like the Positive Reinforcement Method.

Some Tips

  • Keep commands short
  • Stay consistent
  • Always make sure to acknowledge good behavior from bad behavior
  • Make sure not to overreward the dog
  • Training takes time
  • If dog has certain problems specific training is required
  • Reward dogs with stuff like treats and say “good boy”
  • Training styles are different depending on the person and method
