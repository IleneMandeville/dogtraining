General Information

The purpose of the Behavioral dog training
style is to teach the dog to follow
commands. This method is the second type of
training the puppy is exposed to. This type
of training has different levels to it from
basic to advanced commands. There are even
commands for certain tricks.

The Different Command Types

  • Basic Training
  • Advanced Training
  • Tricks

Some Command Usefull Commands

  • Sit: extend an open hand to your dog
  • Stay: hold an extended fist or open palm
    near your dog's head
  • Down: move your hand down
  • Come Here: gesture towards yourself
